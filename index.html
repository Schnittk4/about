Drusilla Schnittke

Drusilla is a sound artist based in Portland, OR. When writing, she depends on mixing different media and styles to get across what is on her mind. Her main mediums are audio programming and more traditional music composition, though she also often relies on visual creative coding and poetry to complement her compositions. Drusilla frequently works on collaborations, usually writing film scores, but, also, working with other artists on installations. Both in her collaborative and solo work, Drusilla explores the blurring of the mental and physical world, allegoric and literal, through the themes of mental illness, neurodivergence and the experience of Russian queerness.
